What is COVID-19?

Covid-19, or the Coronavirus Disease 2019, is an infectious disease first detected in Wuhan, China.


Symptoms

These symptoms would typically appear after 14 days after getting exposed to COVID-19

Transmission


Covid spreads when someone inhales droplets and airborne particles that hold the virus that linger and contaminate the air around.


Transmission can also occur through splashing/spraying contaminated fluids into the eyes, nose or mouth.

Pregnancy response

There are many uncertainties for pregnant women during this pandemic, being more prone to complications and infections from other types of coronaviruses. Making theme a vulnerable group and are advised to take extra preventive measures.

There are some increased rate of unfavourable outcomes during the course of pregnancy which include:

  • Foetal growth restriction
  • Preterm birth
  • Perinatal mortality
